Privacy & data protection
Privacy is about what information you can collect about people, what you can do with it, how you handle it, and how you provide transparency and control to the people whose data it is.
AI & algorithmic risk
AI risk is about what happens when your product uses automated systems to make decisions or predictions that affect users — and who's responsible when those decisions cause harm, even if you didn't build the model yourself.
Online safety
Online safety focuses on protecting individuals, especially vulnerable users, from harm that happens through your product: harassment, illegal content, manipulation, and fraud. If your product has user-generated content, a recommendation system, or any form of user interaction, this likely applies to you.
Children's safety
Children's protection law applies specific, stricter rules to any product that children are likely to use, not just products built for children. If your product could plausibly reach minors, these rules apply whether you intended that or not.
